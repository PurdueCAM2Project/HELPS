We try to solve the following questions:

  1. Does the concentration of the objects of interest in the training data of a model change how well that model will transfer to a new data set with objects of interest concentrated in a different region?
  2. And if so, does augmenting the data to reduce distinctiveness decrease the amount of labels required?
  3. And if so, what is the number of augmented data and label required while not giving up accuracy.
We are in the process of validating how dataset's distinctiveness impacts a model's accuracy meaning validating biases within the datasets.
