Cross-referencing social media and public surveillance camera data for disaster purpose

Best paper award in conference: IEEE HST 2018 category: Humanitarian Assistance and Disaster Relief

Physical media (like surveillance cameras) and social media (like Instagram and Twitter) may both be useful in attaining on-the-ground information during an emergency or disaster situation. We tested whether social media is of utility when physical surveillance cameras went off-line during Hurricane Irma in 2017. Specifically, we collected and compared geo-tagged Instagram and Twitter posts in the state of Florida during times and in areas where public surveillance cameras went off-line. We then analyzed the utility of social media content and frequency for emergency managers or first responders during a natural disaster.
